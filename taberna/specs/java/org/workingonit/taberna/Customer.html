Reservations tests

Let's imagine that the Taberna hotel has opened a new luxury suite (number 777) named Mon Bijou. And both Harry and Sally want to reserve the room for the week 51.

Harry is the first to reserve the suite 777 for the week 51; Harry gets the reservation.

If Sally later reserves the suite 777 for the week 51 then Harry still has still the reservation.

However, Harry has to cancel his reservation. Now if Sally books the suite 777 for the week 51 then Sally will get the reservation.
